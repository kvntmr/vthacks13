Privacy Policy - TechFlow Solutions

1. Introduction
TechFlow Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in accordance with industry standards including ISO 27001, SOC 2, and NIST Cybersecurity Framework.
2. Information We Collect
2.1 Personal Information

Name, email address, and contact information
Account credentials and authentication data
Payment and billing information
Device identifiers and IP addresses
Usage data and analytics

2.2 Technical Information

System logs and performance metrics
Security event data
Browser type and operating system information
API usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information
We process your personal data for the following purposes:

Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our services
Security Monitoring: To detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents
Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements
Analytics: To understand usage patterns and optimize performance
Communication: To send service-related notifications and updates

4. Data Security Measures
4.1 ISO 27001 Compliance
Our Information Security Management System (ISMS) follows ISO 27001 standards, including:

Regular risk assessments and management procedures
Documented security controls and policies
Continuous monitoring and improvement processes
Annual third-party audits and certifications

4.2 SOC 2 Type II Controls
We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance through:

Security: Logical and physical access controls
Availability: System uptime and performance monitoring
Processing Integrity: Data processing accuracy and completeness
Confidentiality: Protection of sensitive information
Privacy: Collection, use, and disposal controls for personal information

4.3 NIST Cybersecurity Framework Implementation
Our cybersecurity program aligns with NIST CSF core functions:

Identify: Asset inventory, risk assessment, and governance
Protect: Access control, data security, and protective technology
Detect: Continuous monitoring and detection processes
Respond: Incident response planning and communications
Recover: Recovery planning and improvements

5. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored in encrypted databases using AES-256 encryption
Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for business purposes
Automated deletion processes remove data according to established schedules
Backup data is maintained with the same security standards as primary data

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your information with:

Service providers and business partners under data processing agreements
Legal authorities when required by law or court order
Third parties with your explicit consent

All data sharing arrangements include appropriate safeguards and contractual protections.
7. Your Rights and Choices
7.1 Access and Control

Request access to your personal information
Update or correct inaccurate data
Request deletion of your personal data
Object to certain processing activities

7.2 Data Portability
You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service provider.
8. Security Incident Response
In the event of a data breach:

We will assess the incident within 24 hours
Affected users will be notified within 72 hours
Regulatory authorities will be notified as required
Post-incident analysis will be conducted to prevent future occurrences

9. Compliance and Auditing

Continuous monitoring of regulatory requirements
